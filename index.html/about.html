The Start of Guru Gluten Free

Years ago i was diagnosed with a gluten sensitity, after not taking it seriously over the years it finally caught up to me and now it feels like full blown celiac. My sister was diagnosed with celiac disease years ago so it runs in the family, i watched her not be able to enjoy family dinners and struggle to enjoy going out to eat with everyone. After i was diagnosed i decided to take this serious and start learning how to cook good homemade meals that are not basic meals. My love for cooking for me and my family turned into people asking me to cook for them and thus to the online store we have now that ships all over the United States.
